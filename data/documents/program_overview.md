# Thrive Scholars Program Overview

## About Thrive Scholars

Thrive Scholars is a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping high-achieving, low-income students access and succeed at top colleges and universities. Our mission is to close the opportunity gap by providing comprehensive support throughout the college journey.

## Our Programs

### Summer Academy
The Summer Academy is an intensive 6-week program that prepares rising high school seniors for the college application process. Students participate in:
- College essay writing workshops
- SAT/ACT preparation
- Interview skills training
- Campus visits to top universities
- Leadership development activities

### College Success Program
Once enrolled in college, Thrive Scholars continue to receive support through:
- Academic tutoring and study groups
- Career mentorship from industry professionals
- Networking events with Thrive alumni
- Financial literacy workshops
- Mental health and wellness resources

### Career Launch
Our Career Launch program helps students transition from college to career by providing:
- Resume and cover letter reviews
- Mock interviews with professionals
- Internship placement assistance
- Job search strategies
- Professional development workshops

## Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for Thrive Scholars, students must:
1. Be a high school junior at the time of application
2. Demonstrate financial need (family income typically below $75,000)
3. Have a minimum unweighted GPA of 3.5
4. Show strong academic potential and leadership qualities
5. Be a U.S. citizen or permanent resident
6. Be committed to attending a 4-year college or university

## Application Process

### Timeline
- Applications open: September 1st
- Application deadline: December 15th
- Interviews: January - February
- Decisions released: March 15th
- Summer Academy: Late June - Early August

### Required Materials
- Completed online application
- High school transcript
- Two letters of recommendation
- Personal essay (500-750 words)
- Family financial information
- SAT or ACT scores (if available)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Is Thrive Scholars free?
A: Yes! All Thrive Scholars programs are completely free for accepted students. This includes the Summer Academy, college support, and career services.

### Q: Can international students apply?
A: Currently, Thrive Scholars is only open to U.S. citizens and permanent residents.

### Q: What colleges do Thrive Scholars attend?
A: Our scholars attend a wide range of selective colleges including Ivy League schools, top public universities, and elite liberal arts colleges.

### Q: How competitive is the application process?
A: We receive thousands of applications each year and accept approximately 150-200 new scholars annually. We look for students who demonstrate academic excellence, leadership potential, and a commitment to their communities.
